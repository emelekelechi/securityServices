# SECURITY SERVICES MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

## INTRODUCTION
Security management is the systematic process of identifying an organization's assets, people, buildings, and information systems and implementing policies, procedures, and technologies to protect them from internal and external threats.

Due to the severe insecurity ravaging our country, this model is designed to tracks government, corporate organization, individuals etc who are eager to hire to had hired security personel for the protection of their lives and property.

It is also designed to ascertained available personel who are ready to be deployed as excort guards, bouncers, spy security personels, infantry guards and corpeorate personel and also an oversight of organizations, individuals that have hired.
